Captain America is a superhero appearing in American comic books published by Marvel Comics.

Created by cartoonists Joe Simon and Jack Kirby, the character first appeared in Captain America Comics #1 (cover dated March 1941) from Timely Comics, a predecessor of Marvel Comics. Captain America was designed as a patriotic supersoldier who often fought the Axis powers of World War II and was Timely Comics' most popular character during the wartime period.

Captain America is a World War II veteran, a founding member of the Avengers, and Earth's first known superhero.
